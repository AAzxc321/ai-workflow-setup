# Ross Simmonds

## Why this expert was selected

Ross Simmonds was selected because his work focuses on B2B SaaS content strategy, content distribution, repurposing, and AI-assisted distribution systems. For this research, the useful angle is what happens after content is created: how it gets distributed, reused, cited, and turned into visibility across search, AI systems, social platforms, communities, and buyer journeys.

## Research angle

Use Ross Simmonds as a source for:

- Content distribution as part of SEO strategy.
- Generative Engine Optimization / GEO.
- AI search visibility and LLM citations.
- Repurposing long-form content into platform-specific assets.
- AI-assisted content distribution workflows.
- Distribution audits and content supply chains.
- The role of communities, social platforms, and third-party mentions in AI visibility.
- Turning content production into a repeatable distribution system.

## How these sources support the research

These sources show Ross Simmonds’s approach to AI-powered SEO content production through distribution, repurposing, GEO, and AI-assisted visibility. The materials are useful for understanding how content becomes discoverable after publication, especially when buyers use Google, AI answer engines, social platforms, communities, and third-party sources to make decisions.

The strongest pattern across these sources is that content production alone is not enough. Ross’s work emphasizes that content needs a distribution system: it should be repurposed, placed across relevant platforms, optimized for both SEO and GEO, and supported by signals that make it easier for humans and AI systems to find, trust, and cite.
