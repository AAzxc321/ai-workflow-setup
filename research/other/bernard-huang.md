# Bernard Huang

## Why this expert was selected

Bernard Huang was selected because he built Clearscope, a widely used SEO content optimization platform, and has been actively publishing and speaking about how SEO changes in the AI search era.

His work is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production because it connects traditional SEO content workflows with Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), AI visibility tracking, query fan-out, brand mentions, AI citations, and prompt-based content discovery.

## Research angle

Use Bernard Huang as a source for:

- Answer Engine Optimization / AEO.
- Generative Engine Optimization / GEO.
- AI search visibility and AI citation tracking.
- Query fan-out as a content roadmap.
- Prompt tracking as the AI-era equivalent of keyword tracking.
- Brand mentions and co-mentions as AI visibility signals.
- The shift from ranking pages to becoming part of AI-generated answers.
- How Clearscope is adapting content optimization workflows for Google, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other AI-driven answer systems.
