# Bernard Huang

## Why this expert was selected

Bernard Huang was selected because his recent work focuses on how SEO content optimization changes when discovery happens through AI-generated answers, citations, brand mentions, and answer engines.

For this research, the useful angle is not his general founder/profile background, but his practical work around Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), query fan-out, AI visibility, prompt tracking, topical authority, and AI citation measurement.

## Research angle

Use Bernard Huang as a source for:

- Answer Engine Optimization / AEO.
- Generative Engine Optimization / GEO.
- AI search visibility and AI citation tracking.
- Query fan-out as a content planning method.
- Prompt tracking as the AI-era version of keyword tracking.
- Brand mentions, co-mentions, and citations as AI visibility signals.
- The shift from ranking pages to being included in AI-generated answers.
- How content teams can adapt SEO workflows for answer systems.
