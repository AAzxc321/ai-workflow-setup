# Gael Breton

## Why this expert was selected

Gael Breton was selected because he is the co-founder of Authority Hacker and has moved from traditional SEO, affiliate marketing, and authority-site building into practical AI and automation workflows for marketers and business owners.

His work is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production because he focuses on operational workflows: using AI agents, Claude Code, automation, SEO tools, Google Search Console data, content gap analysis, ad creation, reporting, and business processes.

## Research angle

Use Gael Breton as a source for:

- AI automation for SEO and content workflows.
- Claude Code for non-technical marketers.
- “Action AI” versus chat-based AI.
- Using AI agents to connect SEO tools, files, reports, and workflows.
- SEO strategy automation through Ahrefs, Google Search Console, competitor data, and content gap analysis.
- AI-assisted content, ad, and creative production.
- The shift from traditional authority-site SEO to AI-powered marketing operations.
- Practical AI systems for small teams and lean marketing businesses.
