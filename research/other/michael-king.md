# Michael King

## Why this expert was selected

Michael King was selected because his work connects technical SEO, information retrieval, machine learning, content strategy, and generative AI. For this research, the useful angle is how AI search systems retrieve, evaluate, synthesize, and cite content — and how SEO content needs to be structured for relevance, passage selection, and machine-mediated visibility.

## Research angle

Use Michael King as a source for:

- Relevance Engineering.
- Generative Engine Optimization / GEO.
- Technical SEO for AI search.
- Retrieval, passage selection, embeddings, and citations.
- How AI search systems fetch, evaluate, and synthesize content.
- Why SEO content needs to be structured for both humans and machines.
- The shift from ranking blue links to being selected inside generated answers.

## How these sources support the research

These sources show Michael King’s approach to AI search as a technical relevance problem. The materials are useful for understanding how content gets selected, cited, and synthesized by AI systems, not just how it ranks in a traditional search results page.

The strongest pattern across these sources is that AI-powered SEO content production needs to be built around retrieval and relevance. Content has to be technically accessible, semantically clear, citation-worthy, and structured in a way that machines can understand and reuse inside generated answers.
